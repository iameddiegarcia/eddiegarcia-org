About Eddie

Eddie Garcia operates at the intersection of public sector workforce transformation, HR technology modernization, data strategy, and creative storytelling. Based in Los Angeles, he leads strategy, technology, and performance initiatives for one of the largest municipal employers in the country.

His work spans enterprise systems design, workforce planning models, AI adoption strategy, and building the tools that make complex organizations work better. He also builds in public — shipping AI-powered tools, knowledge systems, and creative projects under the DIGITO banner.
